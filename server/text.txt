Backend structure for MyJournal.

Database:
Name: `myjournal`
type: `SQLite`
Tables: Models


Models:
- `users`
- `entries`
- `user preferences`
- `reminders`
- `tags`
- `entry tags`
- `categories`
- `media files`

Relationships:
- `users` 
    * creates many `entries`
    * has many `user preferences`
    * sets many `reminders`

-`entries`
    * contains many `media files`
    * belongs to `categories`
    * has many `tags`
    * contains many `entry tags`

- `user preferences`
    * belongs to `users`

- `reminders`
    * belongs to `users`

- `tags`
    * has many `entry tags`

- `entry tags`
    * belongs to `entries`

- `categories`
    * has many `entries`

- `media files`
    * belongs to `entries`


Routes:
- `users`
- `entries`
- `user preferences`
- `reminders`
- `tags`
- `entry tags`
- `categories`
- `media files`
